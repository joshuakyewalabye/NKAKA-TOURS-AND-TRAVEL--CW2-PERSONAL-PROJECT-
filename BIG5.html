Kidepo Valley National Park

Remote, rugged, and wildly beautiful, Kidepo is a hidden gem for serious safari lovers. Its sweeping plains and mountain backdrops host cheetahs, giraffes, and large herds of buffalo, often without another tourist in sight.

Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary

The only place in Uganda to track rhinos on foot, Ziwa offers a thrilling and educational experience in conservation. Walk alongside trained rangers as you observe these powerful animals in a safe, protected environment..
