Murchison Falls

Here, the Nile is forced through a narrow 7-meter gorge, exploding into a thunderous cascade that sends mist and rainbows into the air. It's one of the most powerful waterfalls on Earth and a dramatic centerpiece of the national park.

Sipi Falls

Located on the slopes of Mount Elgon, this trio of waterfalls is a haven for hikers, abseilers, and coffee lovers. The surrounding villages offer immersive coffee tours, while the falls themselves provide stunning views and refreshing spray.

Aruu Falls

Aruu's multi-tiered cascades tumble over rocky steps, creating natural pools and scenic picnic spots. It's a photographer's dream and a peaceful escape in northern Uganda, especially during the rainy season when the falls are in full flow
