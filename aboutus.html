Nkaka Tours & Travel is a tour and travel company based in Kampala, Uganda, specializing in tailor-made tours across Uganda and East Africa. Our multi-lingual guides are passionate professionals ready to make your safari unforgettable.

Our Story

Founded by Ruhamya Anthony, a dedicated and enthusiastic director, Nkaka Tours & Travel was incorporated in 2024. With a strong commitment to quality and consistency, we have become a trusted name in East African travel.
