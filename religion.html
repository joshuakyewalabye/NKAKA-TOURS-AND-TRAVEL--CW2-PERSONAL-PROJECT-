Namugongo Martyrs Shrine

This sacred site commemorates 22 Catholic martyrs who were executed for their faith in 1886. Every June 3rd, thousands of pilgrims gather here for prayer, reflection, and celebration. The shrine's architecture and serene grounds invite quiet contemplation year-round.

Baháí Temple (Kampala)

Set on a hilltop with sweeping views of Kampala, this temple is the only Baháí House of Worship on the African continent. Its tranquil gardens and elegant dome offer a peaceful retreat for visitors of all faiths.

Rubaga Cathedral

A landmark of Uganda's religious heritage, Rubaga Cathedral stands as one of the oldest Catholic churches in the country. Its red-brick façade and stained-glass windows tell stories of faith, resilience, and colonial history.
