Bwindi Impenetrable Forest

Step into a world of misty jungle and ancient trees, where over half of the planet's mountain gorillas reside. Mgahinga Gorilla National Park

Though Uganda's smallest park, Mgahinga punches above its weight in biodiversity and scenic drama. Nestled among the Virunga volcanoes, it offers intimate gorilla encounters alongside golden monkey tracking and panoramic views that stretch across three countries.

Rushaga Sector (Bwindi)

For those seeking deeper connection, Rushaga's gorilla habituation experience allows extended time with gorilla families as they acclimate to human presence. It's a rare opportunity to witness behaviors and interactions that most visitors never see.
