What types of tours do you offer?

We specialize in wildlife safaris, gorilla trekking, cultural experiences, and adventure tours across Uganda and East Africa. Each tour is crafted to showcase the beauty and diversity of the region.

How do I book a tour?

You can book directly through our website, via email, or by calling our customer service team. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred dates and activities.

Do you offer customized itineraries?

Yes! We love tailoring experiences to match your interests, budget, and travel style. Just let us know what you're looking for, and we'll design a personalized itinerary for you.

What is included in the tour package?

Our packages typically include accommodation, transportation, park entry fees, guided activities, and meals as specified. Flights and personal expenses are usually excluded unless stated otherwise.

Is it safe to travel in Uganda?

Absolutely. Uganda is a welcoming and safe destination for tourists. We prioritize your safety by working with experienced guides and maintaining high standards throughout your journey.

What should I pack for a safari?

We recommend lightweight clothing in neutral colors, comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, insect repellent, a hat, and a good camera. For gorilla trekking, sturdy boots and a rain jacket are essential.

Do I need a visa to visit Uganda?

Most travelers require a visa to enter Uganda. You can apply online for an e-visa before your trip. Be sure to check the latest entry requirements based on your nationality.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the tour type and notice period. Generally, cancellations made 30 days before departure are eligible for partial refunds. Please refer to our terms and conditions for full details.

How can I contact you for more information?

You can reach us via email, phone, or through our contact form on the website. Our team is happy to answer any questions and help you plan your dream adventure.
